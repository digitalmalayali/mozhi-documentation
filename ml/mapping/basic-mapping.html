Chillus​

The letters r, n, N, l, L or m at the beginning of a word or after a vowel will form corresponding chillu character. Anusvara (ം) is considered a chillu of മ.

MalayalamMozhiUnicode Character(s)Malayalam ExampleMozhi Example
ർrCHILLU RRഅവർavar
ൻnCHILLU Nഅവൻavan
ൺNCHILLU NNആൺaanh
ൽlCHILLU Lഅവൽaval
ൾLCHILLU LLഅവൾavalh
ംmANUSVARAജിംjim
